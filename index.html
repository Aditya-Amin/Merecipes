Inspiring Innovation in the Middle Eastern Kitchen.

Merecipes is a social recipe platform with thousands of Middle Eastern Recipes and Arabic Cuisine like Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner etc. You will be able to search recipes from Egypt, Morocco, Palestine, Jordan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Lebanon, Turkey, Iraq, Kuwait, and others. Our mission is to attract all Middle Eastern food lovers and allow them to share their master recipes. The idea came from a group of food hobbyist, who are interested in growing this Middle Eastern Food community. Also, we would like to promote healthy recipes and ideas for children. We will be publishing interesting content related to the latest food research, interesting facts, and other relevant topics.
